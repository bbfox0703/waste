movhlps and vmovhlps Instructions

movhlps xmm1, xmm0: Copies the high elements of xmm0 to the low elements of xmm1.

vmovhlps xmm0, xmm1, xmm2: Copies the high elements of xmm2 to the low elements of xmm0, and the high elements of xmm1 to the high elements of xmm0.

movhlps xmm1, xmm0
vmovhlps xmm0, xmm1, xmm2
